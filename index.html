TRAFFIC RULES AND REGULATIONS

Road Safety Awareness Website

Introduction

Traffic rules and regulations are essential for maintaining safety and order on roads. They help prevent accidents, protect lives, and ensure smooth movement of vehicles and pedestrians. Every citizen should follow traffic rules responsibly to create a safer environment for everyone.

Objectives of Traffic Rules

  • Ensure safety of drivers and pedestrians.
  • Reduce road accidents and injuries.
  • Maintain smooth traffic flow.
  • Promote responsible driving habits.
  • Protect public property and lives.

Important Traffic Rules

  • Always wear a helmet while riding a motorcycle.
  • Always wear a seat belt while driving a car.
  • Do not use mobile phones while driving.
  • Never drink and drive.
  • Follow all traffic signs and signals.
  • Maintain speed limits.
  • Use indicators before turning.
  • Keep a safe distance from other vehicles.
  • Give way to emergency vehicles.
  • Stop for pedestrians at zebra crossings.

Traffic Signal Meanings

Common Traffic Signs

Traffic Sign Description
Stop Sign Driver must stop completely.
No Parking Parking vehicles is prohibited.

Road Safety Tips

  1. Always obey traffic signs and signals.
  2. Drive at a safe speed.
  3. Avoid distractions while driving.
  4. Check vehicle condition regularly.
  5. Use mirrors before changing lanes.
  6. Respect pedestrians and cyclists.
  7. Never drive when tired or sleepy.

Benefits of Following Traffic Rules

Following traffic rules helps reduce accidents, saves lives, prevents injuries, improves traffic flow, and creates a disciplined transportation system. Responsible road behavior benefits society as a whole.

Road Safety Awareness

Road safety is a shared responsibility. Drivers, pedestrians, cyclists, and passengers must work together to ensure safe roads. Awareness campaigns, education, and strict law enforcement help improve road safety standards.
